ACID JAZZ

The Beginning

Acid Jazz is a form of Jazz that incorporates elements from not only Jazz, but funk, soul, and hip hop as well.

The roots of Acid Jazz can be traced all the way back to club music in London from the 1980s, specifically from a French-English DJ named "Gilles Peterson", who began to use the phrase "Acid Jazz" as an antithesis to Acid House music that was popular in European Nightclubs.

Acid Jazz in Modern times

Since its creation, Acid Jazz has continued to be a popular form of music, and is widely used in all forms of media

One form of such modern media that uses Acid Jazz is video games. An example of one is the soundtrack of the 2016 game: "Persona 5". The game's soundtrack is often praised as being one of the best soundtracks of all time in a video game, and that is thanks to its use of Acid jazz
